Welcome to Heldorya

Brave adventurer, thou art summoned to Heldorya, a wondrous realm of magic, mystery, and marvels untold. Here, amidst vast lands sculpted by modded wonders, thou may build thine own legacy, explore enchanted biomes, and join a fellowship of like-minded souls. Forge alliances, conquer challenges, and carve thine name into legend.

Our server runs on Forge 1.20.1, crafted meticulously with mods to enrich thy journey — from mystical forests to ancient ruins, from bustling towns to secret passages hidden beneath Valdareth Castle.

The Laws of the Land

  • Respect thy fellows: No griefing, no harassment, and no offensive speech.
  • Fair play: No cheating, exploiting bugs, or unfair advantages.
  • Build with honor: Do not claim lands or builds that are not thine own.
  • Server mods: Use only approved mods; connect only after contacting admins.
  • Trading & economy: Use official shops and the bank for fair trade.
  • Housing: Purchase a home in the village to live near the spawn.
  • Protection: Players who cause damage may be confined in the castle's sotterranei.

Explore the World

Set forth on thy journey and embrace the marvels of Heldorya:

  • Build your own home — claim a humble abode in the village near spawn and live among friends.
  • Traverse mystical lands — discover the wonders crafted with Biomes O’ Plenty and other mods, bringing new life to the realms.
  • Gather rare treasures — hunt beasts, farm rare crops, and trade in the bustling marketplace.
  • Test thy courage — explore the castle's sotterranei and prove thy valor.
  • Ride majestic steeds — borrow horses, mules, and donkeys from the castle’s scuderia to aid thy travels.
